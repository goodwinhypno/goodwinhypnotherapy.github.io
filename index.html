How Can Hypnosis Help Me?

"Live Like A Lion!"

Hypnosis is a state of focused attention and deep relaxation that can help manage symptoms and address the psychological factors of various medical conditions. It is an effective complementary treatment used alongside other therapies like CBT.

Mental Health

  • Anxiety Disorders: Manage stress, panic attacks, and procedural anxiety by retraining brain responses.
  • Phobias: Reframe negative beliefs and use "imaginal exposure" to desensitize fears.
  • PTSD: Safely process traumatic memories and improve emotional regulation.
  • Depression: Improve self-confidence and promote positive outlooks.
  • Tinnitus: Focus the mind on blocking irritating noise from consciousness.
  • Sleep Disorders: Improve sleep quality and restorative slow-wave sleep.

Pain & Medical

  • Chronic Pain: Alter perception of fibromyalgia, arthritis, and back pain for long-term relief.
  • Procedural Pain: Manage pain during medical/dental surgery; can reduce anesthesia needs.
  • Cancer Support: Alleviate side effects like pain, nausea, and vomiting.
  • IBS: "Gut-directed hypnotherapy" targets the brain-gut connection.
  • Migraines: Manage and alleviate the pain from tension and headaches.

Behavior & Habits

  • Smoking Cessation: Address underlying habits and subconscious motivations.
  • Weight Loss: Promote healthier eating habits and weight loss maintenance.
  • Executive Function: Improve work performance, efficiency, and executive management.
  • Athletic Performance: Peak performance change, motivation, and fighting burnout.
  • Addictions: Reframe subconscious motivations by exploring underlying trauma.
  • Bed-Wetting: Successful therapeutic application for enuresis.

Medical Disclaimer: This information is for educational purposes only. For medical advice or diagnosis, consult a professional. Hypnotic susceptibility varies by individual. Hypnosis is most effective when part of a broader treatment plan.
