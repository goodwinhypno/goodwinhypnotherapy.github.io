Hypnosis is a state of focused attention and deep relaxation that can help manage symptoms and address psychological factors across a wide spectrum of conditions.

Mental Health

  • Anxiety & Panic: Retrain brain responses to stress and medical procedures.
  • Phobia Resolution: Reframe negative beliefs through "imaginal exposure."
  • PTSD Support: Safely process memories and improve regulation.
  • Depression: Improve confidence and foster positive outlooks.
  • Tinnitus: Block irritating ringing from conscious awareness.
  • Sleep Quality: Deepen restorative slow-wave sleep.

Pain & Medical

  • Chronic Pain: Manage fibromyalgia, arthritis, and back pain perception.
  • Procedural Ease: Reduce anesthesia needs during dental/medical surgery.
  • Cancer Support: Alleviate pain, nausea, and vomiting from treatments.
  • Gut Health: Target the brain-gut connection for IBS relief.
  • Migraines: Alleviate tension and chronic headache intensity.

Behavior & Habit

  • Smoking Cessation: Address subconscious motivations and triggers.
  • Weight Management: Promote healthier eating habits and weight loss.
  • Performance: Optimize executive management and athletic efficiency.
  • Burnout: Combat fatigue and re-energize goal achievement.
  • Addiction Support: Explore underlying trauma to reframe behavior.
  • Childhood Support: Effective therapeutic application for enuresis.
