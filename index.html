What is Hypnotherapy?

A medically recognized tool for positive change.

A Natural State of Focused Attention

Hypnotherapy is a therapeutic technique that uses guided relaxation and focused attention to achieve a heightened state of awareness, often called a trance. In this natural state, you are more open to suggestions that can help you modify your perceptions, behaviors, sensations, and emotions.

It's important to know: You are always in control during hypnosis and cannot be made to do anything against your will. It is a collaborative process between you and your therapist.
